WHU Seminar: Geometry of wave equation Strichartz estimates

Syllabus: (1) traditional and bilinear Strichartz estimates, original proof by frequency approach, c.f. [Klainerman], Chapter 8-9; (2) tube decomposition, induction on scales, geometry of wave-packet interaction, c.f. [Klainerman-Rodnianksi-Tao].

2024 spring

WHU Seminar: Dispersion of non-linear waves: geometry and frequency

Textbook[Luk]&[Wang]

Syllabus: (1) Sketch of linear results; (2) Energy estimates, local well-posedness and blow-up criterion; (3) Elements of Lorentzian geometry, vector fields and multiplers; (4) Klainerman-Sobolev inequalities, global existence for $n \geq 4$ ; (5) Good derivatives, null condition and global existence for $n=3$ ; (6) Review of Littlewood-Paley theory and oscillatory integrals; (7) Non-endpoint Strichartz estimates, GWP for defocusing subcritical NLW; (8) Space-time resonance, relation to null condition, c.f. [Germain]&[Pusateri].

WHU Seminar: Paradifferential calculus and non-linear PDEs

Textbook[Danchin] Vedio ExtraNote
